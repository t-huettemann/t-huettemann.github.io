MTH1011 - Gauss elimination example generator and training tool v1

General comments

This training tool generates a random system of linear equations and guides you throgh the process of solving it.

Restrictions

The training tool implements a rather strict version of Gauss elimination:

  • You cannot multiply an equation with a number.
  • You cannot swap two equations unless you must do so.
  • In case you have to swap two equations, you must swap the current equation with the next equation further down that has a non-zero coefficient at the relevant position.

Restarting with a new example

To start again with a new example, click the button at any time.
