About

This webpage is under construction

I am a Senior Lecturer in Pure Mathematics at the Mathematical Sciences Research Centre, School of Mathematics and Physics, Queen's University Belfast.

Research interest

My research interests are in algebra, specifically on graded algebra, algebraic \(K\)-theory and homological finiteness conditions.

Administration

  • External examiner for UG Mathematics at Swansea University
  • Exams Liaison Officer

  • Level-1 coordinator for Mathematics and Statistics
  • Computing and publicity officer for MSRC
