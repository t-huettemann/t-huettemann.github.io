About

Updated 2021-10-11 (15:57)

I am a Senior Lecturer in Pure Mathematics at the Mathematical Sciences Research Centre of the School of Mathematics and Physics at Queen's University Belfast.

Research interest

My research interests are in algebra, specifically graded algebra, algebraic K-theory and homological finiteness conditions. I am particularly interested in strongly graded rings, a natural and powerful generalisation of group rings introduced by Dade.

Current involvement

  • External examiner for UG Mathematics at Aberdeen University
  • Erasmus Programme Director for Mathematics and Statistics
  • Level-1 coordinator for Mathematics and Statistics
  • Member of Faculty Research Ethics Committee

Previous roles

  • External examiner for UG Mathematics at Swansea University
  • Exams Liaison Officer for Pure Mathematics
  • Computing and publicity officer for the MSRC
